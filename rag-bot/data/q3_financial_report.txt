# Q3 Financial Report Summary

## Revenue Growth
Our Q3 gross revenue increased by 14% compared to Q2, bringing the total quarterly revenue to $2.4 Million. This growth was primarily driven by the enterprise sales division closing the "MegaCorp" account.

## Headcount & Expenses
We hired 15 new employees in Q3, focusing mostly on the Engineering and Sales departments. Total operational expenses increased by 6%, which is well within our projected safety margins.

## Future Outlook
Based on the current trajectory, we expect Q4 revenue to surpass $3 Million, provided that the new product suite launches on schedule in late November.
