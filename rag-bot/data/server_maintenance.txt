# Server Maintenance Procedures

## Emergency Contacts
If the production server goes down outside of normal business hours, immediately page the Lead System Administrator, Sarah Jenkins, at 555-0198. Do NOT attempt to restart the database cluster without her explicit authorization.

## Scheduled Downtime
Routine server maintenance is scheduled for the first Sunday of every month at 2:00 AM EST. Expected downtime during this window is approximately 45 minutes.

## Backup Rotation
Full backups are performed weekly on Friday nights. Incremental backups are executed every 12 hours. All backup images are stored in the redundant AWS S3 Glacier buckets for compliance purposes.
