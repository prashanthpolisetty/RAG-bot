# Employee Handbook 2026

## Office Hours & Remote Work
Standard office hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM in the user's local timezone. Following the new 2026 guidelines, employees are permitted to work remotely on Tuesdays and Thursdays. 

## Holidays
The company observes all national holidays. In addition, there is a mandatory company-wide shutdown during the last week of December to allow employees to rest. 

## Equipment Policy
All remote workers are eligible for a one-time $500 stipend to setup their home office parameters, including ergonomic chairs and external monitors.
